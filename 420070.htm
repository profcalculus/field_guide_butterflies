Identification

Very small brown butterfly; with Gerald's Brown S. geraldi, the smallest Stygionympha species. Wings more rounded than Robertson's BrownS. robertsoni, with which it flies. It lacks hind wing ocelli on underside. Hind wing underside brown, irrorated with tiny off–white to yellow speckles; row of submarginal spots in same colour.

 

Sexes similar, female more heavily built, with stouter abdomen, and wings more elongated and rounder.

 

Flight fastest of genus (along with S. geraldi); flies close to ground, where small size makes it difficult to follow. Settles seldom, opens wings for an instant before closing them.
